Slang Dictionary

Sigma
Someone independent and self-reliant, who doesn't follow trends or seek validation from others.
Cap
A lie or exaggeration. "No cap" means "no lie" or "for real."
Drip
A stylish, fashionable outfit or look.
Simp
Someone who is overly eager to please another person, often in hopes of gaining their affection.
GOAT
Greatest Of All Time. Used to describe someone who is exceptionally skilled or successful in their field.
Vibe Check
An assessment of the general atmosphere or mood of a situation or person.
Bet
Agreed, okay, or indeed. Can also be used to express excitement or affirmation.
Slay
To do something exceptionally well or impressively; to succeed brilliantly.
Ghosting
Ending a personal relationship by suddenly withdrawing from all communication without explanation.
